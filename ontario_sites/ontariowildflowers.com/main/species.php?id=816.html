Round Leaf Orchid
Platanthera orbiculata

Other common names: Small Round-leaved Orchid

Other scientific names: Lysias orbiculata, Orchis orbiculata

Family: Orchid Family (Orchidaceae)

Group: Orchids

Flowers: Summer;  White;  Irregular flowers

Leaves: Simple, Entire

Habitat: Forests

Native/Non-native: Native

Origin and Meaning of Names:
 Scientific Name: orbiculata: rounded
